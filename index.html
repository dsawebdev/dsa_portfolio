Greetings from DSA

Hi world! I'm Devon Anderson from Athens, Georgia(Go Dawgs!). I am a Freelance Developer with experience creating mobile apps and websites for both myself, and for clients. I have experience programming in many different languages such as JavaScript, PHP, and Swift.

My passion for design and coding has recently taken back seat to another career path that I love, but it's not a true passion. It's time for me to get back to that feeling of pure creativity and regain my passion!

The conscious effort of this portfolio site will for you (the reader, supporter, and potential cleint) to follow along in my journey back to design, coding.

I am still brushing off things of the past that I have learned and I am also challenged to add new technologies that will make me a more modern developer.

My main goal for any cleint is to invoke a great user interface in my designs and gain an emotional user experience in the process. So lets get to it!!
